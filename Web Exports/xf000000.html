06/11/2018: IMA Conservator - Linda Witkowski: Though painting is generally stable, localized areas of retouch are required on the painting prior to display. Once completed the painting must be placed into a microclimate chamber as it is a painting on wood panel. The frame requires localized retouch and must be built up to accommodate a microclimate chamber that will be built to protect the painting. ( Optium glazing, wood spacers, Marvel seal film).
12/07/2010: From Mark Roskill's unpublished catalogue of the Clowes Collection, 1968: "Technically examined at the Fogg Museum, Sept. 1966. Very extensive overpaint, over heavily fluorescent varnish. Repaint in the mouth, over crackle, did not appear under ultra-violet - implying it is under the varnish. The structure of the support is very much like that in the Landscape with Hermit attributed to Pieter Breughel (q.v.)"
